PROJECT: GAMMA – BRAND AD INT. STUDIO SET – DAY TALENT: SUHANI CAMERA: SONY A7C LENS: SIGMA 24–70MM BACKDROP: OFF-WHITE PAPER ROLL
▸ Camera · Lens · Backdrop Notes
Camera
Sony A7C
Lens
Sigma 24–70mm f/2.8
Backdrop
Off-white paper roll throughout. Dark grey on standby for pickups only — it kills the visible-set context needed in wide shots.
Wide shots
24mm
Full set visible — backdrop roll, light stands, boom, chair, cables. Dress the set intentionally before rolling. This is production design, not an accident.
Med. coverage
50mm
Workhorse for all principal dialogue. Flattering perspective, natural compression, f/2.8 gives clean subject separation.
Close-ups
70mm
Reactions and emotional beats. BG compresses and goes soft at f/2.8. Isolates Suhani's face cleanly against the off-white.
ECU (shots 14, 18)
Move the camera physically closer — do not crop in post. The 24–70 won't reach a true ECU from a comfortable distance.
⚠ Distortion
Sigma at 24mm shows barrel distortion on A7C. Apply lens correction profile in Premiere or Lightroom — backdrop lines will bow otherwise.
⚠ Shot 11
Critical shot. Use fluid head or IBIS locked to a single axis. No handheld shake — at 70mm f/2.8 even small movement reads.
⚠ Cards
A7C is single card slot. Have a backup card plan before the shoot day starts.
